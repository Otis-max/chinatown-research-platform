Media Word Cloud Analysis

Analyzing contemporary discourse and themes in Chinatown-related publications

Media Analysis Insights

Based on approximately 100 reports on Chinatowns published worldwide between 2014 and 2024, we used the NLTK natural language processing toolkit and the Word Cloud visualization library in the Python programming language.

Key Findings
  • The core impression focuses on "cultural festivals, community belonging and urban space identity."
  • Chinatown is a carrier of actual living space and ethnic organization.
  • Chinatown is generally presented as a "cultural experience zone".
  • Chinatown is portrayed in the media as a friendly and worthy of experience other cultural space.
